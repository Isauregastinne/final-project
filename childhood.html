Gabrielle Coco Chanel Story

From the scratch to success

 

Gabrielle's childhood is far from glamorous

Gabrielle was born in 1883 from a sick mother and an absent father.When she was twelve years-old, her mother died and she was sent to the convent of Aubazine in the south of France. The convent was founded to care for the poor and rejected,as well as for abandoned and orphaned girls. There she learnt how to sew.
