Gabrielle Chanel's early life

 

 

 

 

Gabrielle was sent to the convent of Aubazine in the south of France. The convent was founded to care for the poor and rejected,as well as for abandoned and orphaned girls. There she learnt how to sew.

 

Gabrielle's childhood, far from glamorous

Gabrielle was born in 1883 from a sick mother and an absent father. When she was twelve years-old, her mother died.
